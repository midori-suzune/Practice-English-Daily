In many countries , traditional foods are being replaced by international fast foods. 
This having a negative effect on both families and societies. To what extend do you agree or disagree 
with this opinion ?

*Solve*

Opinions are divied on whether the growing dominance of international fast food over traditional cuisines is harmfull to families and societies 
To begin with, fast food is a time-saving solution for many households. The increasing demands of modern work life leave parents with littel time or energy to pepare home-cooked meals after a long day and similarly , student and workers with hectic schedules also benefit from being able to grab a quick meal between classes or shifts. Furthermore , fast food is also considerably budget-friendly for many families. Chains such as KFC or Lotteria
regularly offer combo meals at very low prices, making them a highly attractive dinning opinion for individuals and families living on a tight budget 

However , I am convinced that these benefits are far outweighed by the drawbacks. On one hand , the regular consumption of high-fat , high-sugar food leads to rising rates of obesity and chronic diseases such as diabetes and heart conditions among the general population. This , in turn , places a considerable burden on national healthcare systems , as goverments are required to allocate more funding to treat diet-related illnesses that could otherwise have been prevented through healthier eating habits. On the other hand , the widespread popularity of fast food is gradually casuing traditional food culture to disappear. As younger generations grow up eating burgers and fried chicken rather than tradational home-cooked dishes, they become increasingly unfamiliar with their culinary heritage , causing these cherished repices and time-honoured cooking traditions to gradually die out with older generations.

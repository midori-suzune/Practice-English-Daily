Some may wonder why tiny homes are so darned narrow. Well , like a lot of things in life , it's actually for a very practical reason: because tiny houses  are built on wheeled trailers bases. They often have to built no more than 8.5 feet wide in order for them to be towed on the road without a special permit.

But as anyone who is lived in a small space will tell you ,even an extra few inches can make a huge differences
